Scenario 2B adaptability half-life. Bars report, averaged over 202 full-window latent-oracle switch events, the number of periods required for each method's smoothed excess-risk gap to fall by half after a latent oracle switch; lower values indicate faster adaptation. Finite bars are averaged over events that reach this threshold within the event window. The x-axis is capped at 40 periods; labels printed to the right of capped bars report the uncapped mean. Entries marked not reached are undefined within the event window and should not be interpreted as zero.
