Scenario 2B adaptability profile. Lines show cumulative sums of mean excess latent risk after full-window latent oracle switches, averaged over 50 simulation replications using 202 full-window events out of 307 detected switch events for the ordered set of benchmark and combination methods. These are area summaries, so near-linear curves indicate nearly constant mean horizon-level excess risk rather than a linear recovery path; lower values indicate lower accumulated adaptation cost.
